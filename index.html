Our Story

A Mumbai Icon,
Redefined

Nestled in the heart of Vile Parle, just minutes from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, T24 Retro is more than a hotel — it is a culinary destination. We blend vintage Mumbai soul with contemporary luxury, offering an eclectic experience for both the seasoned traveller and the discerning local diner.

Our in-house restaurant Red Grill celebrates authentic Indian flavours, while the Asian Blend Kitchen takes guests on a pan-Asian voyage. Whether it's a sunrise breakfast, power lunch, or a candlelit dinner, every meal at T24 is a memory in the making.
